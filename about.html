About CareBridge

CareBridge was established in 2020 with the goal of building a modern version of a home care service provider.

Traditional caregiving hasn’t changed for decades, but families today need flexibility, trust, and quick support. It’s a matching problem—what one family needs doesn’t always align with caregiver schedules and location.

At CareBridge Kenya, we provide compassionate, reliable, and personalized home care for your loved ones. Our services range from nursing support and elderly companionship to personal care, disability assistance, and in-home physiotherapy.

We focus on more than just care; we bring comfort, dignity, and peace of mind to families by ensuring clients feel safe, supported, and valued in the comfort of their own homes. With CareBridge, you’re not just getting a caregiver, you’re gaining a trusted partner in health and wellness.
