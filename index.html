About CareBridge

Started in 2020, CareBridge aims to revolutionize home care services in Kenya. We understand that modern families need flexibility, trust, and quick support when it comes to caregiving.

Our mission is to provide reliable, professional, and compassionate care services while making the process of finding and scheduling care as seamless as possible.
